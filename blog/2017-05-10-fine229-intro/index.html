Introduction

May 10, 2017

I’ve always liked “doing art,” but hadn’t understood why.

It was one of my favorite subjects, and I did classes out of school. However, in academics I often got bored… so eventually turned to art on my own time: http://malsatas.deviantart.com/.

Recently I’ve understood that I like “doing art” because I enjoy creating and telling stories. I like doing the work of building worlds and presenting characters - in flavorful and compelling situations. I’ve changed how I approach art and now have new projects that I’m excited to start working on - games and webcomics. The pipe dream is to build public, interactive art.
