Shipping as a habit

Jan 2022 in craft , life , code , art

I’ve worked on a lot of projects (3d printing, grooming habits, video calling), but don’t publish enough. I don’t do the last 5%, I don’t risk it for the biscuit, and I don’t get feedback. The only reliable way to build behavior is through habits. Shipping is certainly a behavior. The answer then, is to make shipping a habit.

I’ll commit to publishing a small thing every day, even if it’s a working note or reflection. Use the namesake of this site for simple accountability.
