Shipping as a habit

Jan 2022 - craft life code art

I’ve worked on a lot of projects (3d printing, grooming habits, video calling), but don’t publish enough. I don’t get to the critical point of trying to deliver what I’ve created into a real market. I don’t do the last 5%, I don’t risk it for the biscuit, and I don’t get enough feedback.

In parallel, I’ve recently been learning through experience that the only reliable way to build behavior is through habits. Shipping is certainly a behavior. The answer then, might be to make shipping a habit.

I’ve committed to publishing a small thing every day, even if it’s a working note or reflection. Using the namesake of this site for simple accountability.
